My work

My design style blends elegance and creativity, with a deep appreciation for the beauty of simplicity. I gravitate toward refined serif typefaces, soft pastel color palettes, and carefully crafted mockups that bring each project to life. Whether I'm sketching by hand or designing digitally, my focus is on creating thoughtful visuals that tell a story, evoke emotion, and reflect intentional detail in every element.

About me

From Brazil to the World

I'm a Brazilian creative with a passion for design, drawing, and bringing ideas to life. I graduated in Marketing and Social Media at Greystone College and further expanded my skills in Web Design at Humber College.

My work is driven by imagination, purpose, and a love for visual storytelling. Currently, I also volunteer with the Media Team at Lagoinha Toronto Church, where I continue to explore and grow my creative talents in a community I love.
